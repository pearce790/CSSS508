Attribute_Label: DSTNUM
Attribute_Definition:
School district long number consistent with the numbering system provided for by the Washington Administrative Code (WAC) and the superintendent of publc instruction. Including the two digit prefix for county identifier makes all values in DSTNUM unique. The prefix for King County is 17 and for Pierce County; 27. This makes Northshore School District 417 (DSTNUM 17417) distinguishable from Pierce County Fife School District 417 (DSTNUM 27417), which overlaps into King County.
